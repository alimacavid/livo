# Junk Removal

Read root CLAUDE.md and divisions/CLAUDE.md first.

## Scope

- Residential junk removal: furniture, appliances, electronics, general clutter
- Estate cleanouts
- Garage and basement cleanouts
- Construction debris removal (small loads)
- Donation drop-off and recycling where possible

## Primary Market

- Homeowners
- Landlords (between tenants)
- Real estate agents (estate sales, listings)
- Businesses doing office cleanouts

## Pricing

See docs/pricing.md. Quoted by volume (truck load fractions).

## Key Competitors

- 1-800-GOT-JUNK
- See notes/competitors.md for full profiles

## Equipment

Truck with enclosed box or open trailer. Dollies, straps, gloves. See docs/equipment.md.

## SOPs

See docs/SOPs.md. Always photograph before/after. Separate recyclables. Never enter crawlspaces or attics.
