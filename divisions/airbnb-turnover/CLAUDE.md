# Airbnb Turnover

Read root CLAUDE.md and divisions/CLAUDE.md first.

## Scope

- Full turnover clean between guest stays
- Linen change and restock
- Restocking consumables on request
- Same-day and next-morning turnarounds
- Photo inspection report after each clean

## Primary Market

- Airbnb and VRBO hosts in Lower Mainland
- STR property managers
- Co-hosting clients (future: LIVO Stays Ltd.)

## Why This Division Leads Growth

- Highest repeat frequency of all divisions
- Locked-in recurring revenue per host
- Natural upsell to deep clean, carpet, windows
