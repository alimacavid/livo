# Vorx — Internal Workforce Platform

INTERNAL ONLY. Never expose Vorx to the public.
Public-facing: LIVO Careers page (marketing funnel only) directs workers to Vorx.

## What Vorx Does
- Worker onboarding and document collection
- Availability management
- Scheduling
- Performance ratings and reviews
- Payment processing

## Key Principle
Multi-skilled workers = more jobs covered per shift.
Workers trained across multiple LIVO divisions = higher utilization.

## Access
- Platform: vorx.ca
- Careers page on livoland.com directs applicants here
- No Vorx branding or internals visible to clients
