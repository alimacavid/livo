# LIVO Strategy and Growth Notes

1. Activate LandEx pipeline: cross-sell LIVO to existing demolition/construction clients. Zero marketing spend required.
2. Generate LIVO cash flow: prove each division, build reviews, refine pricing.
3. Build Holdco structure: Canadian Holdco with LandEx + LIVO as subsidiaries.
