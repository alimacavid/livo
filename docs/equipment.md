# LIVO Equipment Master List

## Division 1 — Power Washing & Window Cleaning
- Pressure washer (commercial-grade, 4000 PSI+)
- Surface cleaner attachment
- Extension wands
- Window squeegees (Ettore or Unger)
- Pure water pole system
- Microfibre sleeves and buckets
- Safety harness (for multi-storey)

## Division 2 — Moving
- Mercedes Sprinter 3500 (primary vehicle)
- Moving blankets (commercial)
- Dollies (appliance + furniture)
- Stretch wrap and tape
- Ratchet straps
- Wardrobe boxes

## Division 3 — Carpet Cleanup
- Mytee LTD12 Escape carpet extractor
- Pre-spray solution (Prochem or Bridgepoint)
- Upholstery tool attachment
- Grooming brush
- Drying fans

## Division 4 — Duct Cleanup
- HEPA-filtered negative air machine
- Flexible duct rods and brushes
- Inspection camera
- Fogging machine (sanitizer)
- PPE: N95, gloves, coveralls

## Division 5 — Crawl Space Cleanup
- Vapor barrier (6-mil poly)
- Insulation removal vacuum
- Dehumidifier (commercial)
- Spray foam gun
- Full PPE (Tyvek suit, respirator, gloves)

## Division 6 — Interior Cleaning
- ProTeam backpack vacuum (HEPA)
- Rubbermaid commercial mop system
- Diversey or Ecolab chemical line
- Microfibre flat mop heads
- Steam cleaner

## Division 7 — Landscaping
- Commercial mower
- String trimmer and edger
- Leaf blower (backpack)
- Hand tools (rakes, shovels, pruners)
- Dump trailer

## Division 8 — Junk Removal
- Dump trailer (primary)
- Sprinter van (overflow)
- Heavy-duty gloves and dollies
- Tarp and tie-downs
